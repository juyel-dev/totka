🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Short version: We store only what you give us (username, bio, encoded file text). Your files are never uploaded to our servers — they are encoded entirely in your browser. We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. We don't track you.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Display name and username
  • Email address or phone number (used as login ID)
  • Password (stored as a SHA-256 hash — never in plain text)
  • Optional: bio, profession, profile picture, social media links

When you upload a file, we store:

  • The encoded text representation of your file (not the raw file)
  • Post title, description, tags, and alt text you provide
  • Original file size (as a human-readable string, e.g. "2.4 MB")
  • Timestamp of upload

2. How Your Files Are Handled

Your files are never uploaded to any TOTKA server. All encoding happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Only the resulting text string (the encoded output) is sent to our Google Sheets database via Google Apps Script.

We cannot decrypt or read the content of your files. Encoded data appears as a stream of characters that is meaningless without the TOTKA decoding engine.

3. Storage Infrastructure

All data is stored in Google Sheets spreadsheets managed by the TOTKA administrator. By using TOTKA, your data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy as well as this policy.

Google Apps Script Web Apps process your requests. No third-party databases or analytics services are used.

4. Profile Pictures

Profile pictures are compressed to a maximum of 50KB using the Canvas API in your browser before being stored. Pictures are stored as base64-encoded strings in Google Sheets. They are publicly visible on your profile page.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

TOTKA does not use tracking cookies. We use:

  • localStorage — to store your login session token (token, user ID, username, profile pic). Cleared on logout.
  • sessionStorage — to cache feed pages temporarily (cleared when you close the tab).
  • localStorage — to store your recent decode history (Post IDs only, stored on your device, never sent to us).

6. NSFW Content

Users who mark content as NSFW (18+) take responsibility for doing so accurately. NSFW content is hidden behind a blur filter by default and requires explicit user action to reveal. The platform administrator reserves the right to remove NSFW content that violates the Terms of Service.

7. Admin & Notifications

The platform administrator receives Telegram notifications for new user signups, post uploads, and system events. This includes your username, user ID, and post details — but never your password or file contents.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your own posts from your profile page at any time. To request complete account deletion, contact the administrator. Note that encoded file chunks in storage sheets may not be fully removed even after post deletion (see the About page for technical details on why).

9. Children's Privacy

TOTKA is not intended for users under 13 years of age. NSFW content on TOTKA is strictly for users aged 18 and over. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact the administrator.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of TOTKA after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
